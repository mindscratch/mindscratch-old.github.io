Go Resources

by Craig Wickesser - Sep 1st, 2013 - posted in go

Recently I’ve been taking a look at Go by Googlers. Go looks like a very interesting language because of its relatively small syntax, fulfilling standard library and great documentation.

Here’s a few links I’ve bookmarked recently that I thought I’d share for anyone else getting started or perhaps for myself to look back at when I’m a Go Guru :)

  • http://godoc.org/ – Go documentation
  • http://talks.golang.org/2012/splash.article
